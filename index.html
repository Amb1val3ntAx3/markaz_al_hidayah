—Naxwaha—

Naḥw (Arabic grammar) teaches how words fit together to form clear, meaningful sentences. It’s essential for understanding the Qur’an, Hadith, and classical Arabic texts with accuracy and depth.

—Tafseerka—

Tafseer is the art of interpreting the Qur’an, providing clarity on its meanings, context, and divine guidance. By studying Tafseer, you gain deeper insights into the verses of the Qur’an, understanding its timeless message and how to apply its teachings in your life.
